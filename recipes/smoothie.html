Peanut Butter and Banana Protein Smoothie

Description

With a busy day ahead of you, enjoy a high protein snack that will keep you going you for the rest of the morning.

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder of choise (Vanilla or Chocolate)
  • Peanut Butter
  • Banana
  • Milk or Water

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients into blender
  2. Blend until desired consistency
  3. Pour and enjoy!
