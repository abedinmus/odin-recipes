Apple Cinnamon Cottage Cheese Bowl

Description

Want to an enjoy an easy but filling and nutritious breakfast? This easy 4 ingredient breakfast bowl recipe can be made within minutes and is perfect for the busy person.

Ingredients

  • Low Fat Cottage Cheese
  • Apple
  • Cinnamon
  • Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Cut apple into bite sized pieces
  2. Put apple pieces in a bowl with serving of cottage cheese
  3. Sprinkle some Cinnamon poweder
  4. Drizzle over some maple syrup
  5. Mix and Enjoy!
